Blue Footed Booby

The Galapagos are home to the Worlds largest concentration of Blue-Footed Boobies.

  • Scientific Name: Sula nebouxii
  • Average Length: 32 in long
  • Average Lifespan : 17 years
  • Habitat: The blue-footed booby is distributed among the continental coasts of the eastern Pacific Ocean from California to the Galapagos Islands south into Peru

Not only does the Blue Footed Booby have a unique and eye-catching name, the birds feet alone make it one of the coolest marine birds out there!
